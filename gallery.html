Photos With Names

This gallery presents a curated collection of historic photographs from Kinghorn, Scotland. Each image has been carefully restored and, where possible, annotated with the names of the people shown, along with dates, places, and contextual details. The photographs span schools, families, workplaces, clubs, and everyday life from the late nineteenth century through to the later twentieth century. The gallery is read-only and continuously updated as new identifications are confirmed. Use the search box to explore by surname or by year. Selecting a year shows all known photographs from that period, while searching by name highlights images in which that person is identified. Click any image to view it full size.
