About this Site

This website is a long-term, independent record of the history, people, places, industries and everyday life of Kinghorn. It has been developed and maintained over many years using original photographs, documents, and personal recollections, with an emphasis on accuracy, transparency, and preservation. The site is intentionally static and lightweight, published via GitHub Pages to maximise longevity and ease of maintenance. All content is curated rather than crowd-edited. While it is actively maintained, it is also structured so that it could, if necessary, be taken over by another technically competent custodian with an interest in local history and digital preservation.
