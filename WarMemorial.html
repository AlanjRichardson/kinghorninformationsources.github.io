Kinghorn War Memorial – Roll of Honour

The following names are taken from Kinghorn War Memorial and record the men of Kinghorn who gave their lives in the World Wars and the Korean War.

You can click on a highlighted name to see their Epitaph.

If you have an epitaph you would like to publish for any of your kin, who are named here, please send me the details with a photo if you have one

The Historical Society have published a book Kinghorn Remembers 1914 - 1919 compiled and edited by Ginny Reid. This book contains brief biographies of the WW1 men listed on Kinghorn War Memorials.
